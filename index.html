For Educators

We create customized AR applications for educators and schools, transforming traditional learning into immersive, interactive experiences. From virtual science experiments and 3D models of historical landmarks to interactive storytelling and hands-on problem-solving, our AR apps enhance engagement and make complex concepts easier to understand. For Architects

We develop tailored AR applications for architects, enabling clients to explore designs like never before. From 1:1 real-life walkthroughs of buildings to interactive features like material and furniture customization, our apps make presentations more engaging and decision-making seamless. Designed for Android and iOS, our solutions combine innovation with practicality to elevate your architectural projects
